關於 About Yat Ji Cha 一字・一杯・一真相

· Origin · 緣起 ·

Somewhere in the fog of Sheung Wan, between a dim sum cart and the last tram of the evening, there exists a tea house that does not appear on any map. The Old Man who runs it does not advertise. He does not need to.

People come when they are troubled. Or restless. Or when they have a feeling they cannot name. They push open the door, sit down, and speak one word. Just one. And the Old Man listens — not to the word, but to what lives beneath it.

一字茶，不是飲品，而是對話。不是答案，而是一個空間——讓你的感受有地方落腳。

茶

· What It Is · 這是什麼 ·

Yat Ji Cha is an AI-powered contemplative experience rooted in Cantonese tea culture. You arrive with a single word — a feeling, a name, a question — and receive in return a quiet reflection brewed from old Hong Kong wisdom, delivered with the warmth of a late-night tea house.

It is not therapy. It is not fortune-telling. It is a pause. A breath. The kind of conversation that used to happen between strangers over pu-erh at 11pm on a rainy Tuesday.

你帶一個字來，我們把它泡開，看看裡面藏著什麼。

I · Abstract

The Philosophy of the Single Word 一字哲學

Yat Ji Cha (一字茶, "One Character Tea") is a contemplative AI experience rooted in Cantonese cultural heritage. Users submit a single word — a feeling, an emotion, a name — and receive in return a reflective response crafted to evoke the wisdom of a Hong Kong tea house. This document outlines the design philosophy, technical architecture, cultural framework, and roadmap of the project.

一字茶係一個沉思性人工智能體驗，植根於廣東文化遺產。用戶提交一個字——一種感受、一種情緒、一個名字——並以香港茶室智慧風格的反思性回應作為回報。

「一字見心，萬語難言。」

One character reveals the heart — ten thousand words cannot.

II · Problem Statement

The Loss of Slow Conversation 慢語言的失落

Modern digital communication is fast, surface-level, and optimised for engagement over depth. Emoji replace nuance. Notifications replace contemplation. In Hong Kong specifically, a city with one of the world's richest oral traditions — Cantonese, a tonal language with nine distinct tones — the texture of spoken feeling is being eroded by shorthand.

There exists a gap between what people feel and what they can express. Yat Ji Cha is an attempt to fill that gap, not with more words, but with a single, carefully chosen one.

現代數位通訊快速、表面，並針對參與度而非深度進行優化。表情符號取代了細微差別。通知取代了沉思。一字茶嘗試填補這個空白。

III · Design Principles

How the Tea House Thinks 茶室的思考方式

The AI persona — the Old Man of the tea house — is designed around three constraints: brevity, warmth, and cultural specificity. Every response must be short enough to fit on a paper receipt. Every response must feel like it was spoken, not written. Every response must carry the scent of Sheung Wan on a rainy evening.

Principle 原則 Implementation
Brevity 簡潔 Responses capped at three sentences
Cultural Grounding 文化根基 Cantonese proverbs in every response
Emotional Safety 情感安全 No advice — only reflection
Aesthetic Coherence 美學一致性 Rain, ink, amber, silence
Language Duality 語言雙重性 Chinese characters + English prose

IV · Technical Architecture

The Kitchen Behind the Counter 櫃台後面的廚房

Yat Ji Cha is a fully client-side web application. There is no backend server, no database, and no persistent storage of user inputs. The AI responses are generated in real-time via the Anthropic Claude API, using a carefully engineered system prompt that instructs the model to assume the persona of the Old Man and respond in a structured three-part format.

The visual experience — rain canvas, amber glows, Noto Serif typography — is constructed entirely in HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks. No libraries. The philosophy of the interface mirrors the philosophy of the product: minimal, direct, present.

一字茶係一個完全客戶端的網頁應用程式。沒有後端伺服器，沒有數據庫，也沒有用戶輸入的持久存儲。

V · Cultural Framework

Tea House as Sacred Space 茶室作為神聖空間

In Cantonese culture, the tea house (茶樓) is not merely a restaurant. It is a civic institution. A space of democracy — where a factory worker and a banker share the same dim sum cart. Where the elderly teach the young how to knock on the table twice to say thank you without speaking. Where arguments are settled and alliances are made over pots of pu-erh.

Yat Ji Cha seeks to carry this spirit into digital space. Not as nostalgia, but as a living practice. A reminder that depth of feeling deserves space and slowness.

在廣東文化中，茶樓不僅僅是餐廳。它是一個公民機構。一字茶試圖將這種精神帶入數位空間。

VI · Roadmap

What Steeps Next 下一步

  • Phase I Launch · 啟動Single-word contemplation experience. Web-only. English + Traditional Chinese.
  • Phase II Memory · 記憶Optional journal — a private book of words you have brought to the tea house over time.
  • Phase III Community · 社群Public wall of words — anonymised, visual, allowing others to see what the city is feeling.
  • Phase IV Dialect Expansion · 方言擴展Support for Cantonese romanisation (Jyutping), Mandarin, and eventually other Chinese dialects.
  • Phase V Physical Presence · 實體空間Pop-up tea house installations in Hong Kong, combining the digital experience with actual tea service.
